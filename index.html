About Us

Quality, affordable housing — done properly.

At Daarim Property, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality, affordable housing. Based in the heart of Medway, we specialise in sourcing, renovating, and managing properties that offer real value to tenants and wider stakeholders alike.

Our mission is simple: to revitalise properties and communities, one property at a time. Whether you are a local authority or organisation with a Relief Duty, seeking local, affordable and well‑maintained accommodation, an out‑of‑area professional in need of accommodation during a project, or an investor looking for hands‑free property management, Daarim Property delivers tailored solutions with integrity, transparency and care.

What We Do

Prioritising local people, local needs.

We are aware of the shortage of affordable housing in Medway/Kent and the increasing need for emergency and temporary housing for vulnerable adults and families. We are also aware of the rising number of other Boroughs encroaching on the area, taking up the already limited affordable accommodation. We believe Medway residents should be prioritised.

We work in close partnership with local authorities, other landlords and investors to source, renovate and manage accommodation for our key demographic — disadvantaged and vulnerable individuals and families in need of emergency and/or temporary accommodation.

We work closely with local partners, tradespeople, and resources (wherever possible) to transform and maximise underused spaces into suitable and safe living environments.

We also proudly offer our expertise in property solution and management, extending our services to include high‑quality serviced accommodations for out‑of‑area professional contractors while they complete their project(s) in the area.

Driven by integrity and rooted in the local community, our dedication to providing safe and affordable accommodation has guided us since day one. Our commitment to excellence remains unwavering as we strive to support the various housing needs in the community.

Our Services

Tailored accommodation solutions.

At Daarim Property, we provide tailored accommodation solutions that support both social responsibility and professional needs.

Working closely with local authorities, we offer emergency and temporary housing to assist in fulfilling their homelessness relief duties with care, speed, and dignity. For contractors and professionals working on local projects, our high‑quality short‑let and serviced accommodation ensures comfort and convenience close to the job site.

Backed by sound working knowledge of homelessness prevention — its logistical and other complexities and time sensitivity — we deliver seamless, responsive support, making housing one less thing to worry about.

Our proactive property management includes a comprehensive cleaning service, maintenance, and repairs, subject to contract type.

We are committed to excellence and client satisfaction. Trust us to deliver responsive, efficient, and compassionate housing solutions that make a real difference.
